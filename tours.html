Shenzhen Route 1 — DJI One-Day Tour

A technology route focused on DJI flagship experiences, autonomous driving, robotics, and a themed dining stop.

  • 10:00–12:00: DJI Flagship Store at OCT Harbour. Learn about DJI history, products, brand knowledge, and optional hands-on drone trial flight.
  • 12:00–14:00: Drone delivery lunch. A tech-oriented lunch experience centered on app-based ordering and everyday innovation.
  • 14:00–15:00: Driverless car experience with at least 30 minutes of real self-driving ride time.
  • 15:30–17:30: Cutting-edge robot store visit with robot shows, robot dogs, interactive games, and home/public service robots.
  • 18:00–19:30: Dinner at a tech-themed robot restaurant where robots deliver meals.

Shenzhen Route 2 — DJI Sky City Route

A shorter premium version that combines DJI retail, futuristic dining, autonomous driving, and a headquarters visit.

  • 10:00–12:00: DJI Flagship Store at OCT Harbour.
  • 12:30–14:30: Lunch at a tech-themed robot restaurant.
  • 14:30–15:30: Driverless car experience.
  • 16:00–17:30: DJI Sky City headquarters visit, including entry and first-floor exhibition access.

Guangzhou Route — One-Day City Tour

A heritage-and-city route mixing Lingnan culture, Cantonese food, architecture, shopping, skyline views, and a river cruise.

  • 09:00–11:30: Yongqing Fang and Cantonese Opera Art Museum.
  • 11:45–13:00: Tao Tao Ju heritage teahouse lunch.
  • 13:15–15:15: Chen Clan Academy with Lingnan architecture and folk art.
  • 15:30–17:45: Beijing Road Pedestrian Street for snacks, shopping, and pre-sunset city walking.
  • 18:00–19:45: Canton Tower for sunset and night skyline views.
  • 20:00–21:00: Pearl River Night Cruise.

Qingyuan Route — One-Day Adventure Tour

An outdoor route with transport, scenic dining, glass bridge views, cliff roller coaster, and canyon speed coaster experience.

  • 08:30–11:30: Drive to Qingyuan with guide briefing.
  • 11:30–12:30: Local Cantonese lunch featuring Qingyuan chicken.
  • 12:30–14:00: Qingyuan Glass Bridge and mountain views.
  • 14:00–15:30: Cliff Roller Coaster.
  • 15:30–16:30: Canyon Speed Coaster.
  • 16:30 onward: Drive back to Shenzhen.

Jiangmen Route — Two-Day Cultural Tour

A two-day heritage route combining watchtower culture, pastry-making, Chikan Ancient Town, Kaiping watchtowers, and Liyuan Garden.

  • Day 1, 10:00–12:00: Cangdong Heritage Base, watchtower culture briefing, Chinese calligraphy, and traditional pastry workshop.
  • Day 1, 15:00–21:30: Chikan Ancient Town with immersive cultural performances such as lion dance, iron flower show, and folk music.
  • Day 2, 09:00–12:00: Kaiping Watchtowers tour with historical explanation.
  • Day 2, 14:00–16:00: Liyuan Garden and Lingnan-West fusion architecture.

Foshan Route — Kung Fu Culture One-Day Tour

A culture route linking Foshan ancestral heritage, Lingnan lifestyle, and an authentic Wing Chun experience.

  • 07:30–09:30: Depart Shenzhen for Foshan by private car.
  • 09:30–12:00: Foshan Ancestral Temple, lion dance performance, Wong Fei-hung Memorial Hall, and Ip Man Hall.
  • 12:00–15:00: Lingnan Tiandi for lunch, strolling, shopping, and photos.
  • 15:00–16:00: Wing Chun Kung Fu experience with a professional instructor.
  • 16:00–18:00: Return to Shenzhen.
